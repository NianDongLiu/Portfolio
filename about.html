About Me

I specialize in game design with a focus on narrative design, level design, and audio design. I also have experience in 3D animation, character rigging, environment lighting, rendering, and visual effects. My toolkit includes Unreal Engine 5 and Godot for game development, Twine and Figma for narrative design, Maya and Adobe After Effects for modeling and animation, Audacity and Reaper for audio, and Houdini for visual effects.

I am a senior student at Purdue University majoring in Game Development and Animation/Visual Effects, graduating in 2026. My design philosophy is simple: create games from the player's perspective.
